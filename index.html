About HealthTrack

The escalating behavioral health crisis in the United States, exacerbated by the COVID-19 pandemic, has highlighted the urgent need for preventive measures to address the surge in mental health problems and rising rates of fatal drug overdoses. In response to this crisis, Monserrat Estrada-Martin, an Ecology and Evolutionary Biology (EBIO) graduate from the University of Colorado Boulder, and Devon Quispe, a Computer Science student with a focus on pre-medicine, have collaborated to develop a groundbreaking solution: the HealthTrack model. By integrating substance use monitoring into routine healthcare procedures and fostering a culture shift around substance use, HealthTrack aims to revolutionize the healthcare field's approach to this pressing issue.

HealthTrack Insights

AI Risk Screening

HealthTrack harnesses the power of automation, AI risk screening, and self-reported patient data to enhance the patient-provider relationship and drive positive health outcomes. Our advanced AI risk screening analyzes extensive datasets, empowering clinicians to identify potential risks and tailor treatment plans with precision.

Patient Risk Calculations

By automating risk calculations, HealthTrack effortlessly generates vital insights, granting providers easy access to essential information for informed decision-making.

Encouraging Patient Initiative

Moreover, by fostering patient-provider trust, HealthTrack encourages patients to actively participate in their own care. Through self-reported data, patients take the initiative, sharing valuable information that complements clinical assessments.
