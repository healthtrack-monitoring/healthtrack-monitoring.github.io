Devon Quispe

A visionary software developer and AI researcher driven to merge the realms of medicine and technology. With his unwavering dedication and pioneering spirit, Devon is paving the way for a future where technology acts as a catalyst for positive societal shifts, enabling a more compassionate and effective approach to substance use in medicine.
